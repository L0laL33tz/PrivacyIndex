PrivacyIndex is a non-exhaustive, work in progress archive of threats, tools and topics to help you stay private online. If you are new to online privacy, start by defining your threat vectors or begin by exploring different cases. Use the green bubbles to navigate.

PrivacyIndex exists to give you a broad overview of the tools available, the topics that may be important to you, and the threats that may arise. In privacy there is no one fits all, so we make no recommendations as to which tools you should be using. Features are not endorsements.

Please note that we make no guarantees for the information on this page. If you feel like content is missing, outdated, incomplete or incorrect, please consider contributing on GitHub.
