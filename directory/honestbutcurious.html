Mullvad VPN

Mullvad believes in a free internet. Free from mass surveillance and censorship. Free from big data markets and authorities mass monitoring your every step. Mullvad believes in a free internet where everyone has the right to privacy.

Mullvad keeps no activity logs, does not ask for personal information, and even encourages anonymous payments via cash or one of the cryptocurrencies accepted. Your IP address is replaced by one of Mullvad's, ensuring that your device's activity and location are not linked to you. Mullvad accepts payments in Bitcoin.

It is important to note that a VPN can be an honest but curious adversary itself as a VPN provider knows your IP address and the sites you are visiting. Instead, using a VPN should be seen as shifting risk away from your Internet Service Provider. As any company, a VPN provider can be made to give out or collect logs of your browsing activity via law enforcement.
